Threat & Prevention

Deforestasi dan Kerusakan Habitat

Indonesia's forests are under threat from deforestation and habitat loss, which have severe consequences for the environment and wildlife. We are committed to implementing sustainable forestry practices and protecting our natural heritage.

Pencegahan dan Perlindungan

We are actively working to prevent further environmental damage and protect the delicate ecosystems of Indonesia. Through conservation efforts, education, and partnerships, we aim to preserve the beauty and diversity of our islands for generations to come.
